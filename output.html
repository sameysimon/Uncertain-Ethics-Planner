Description

Hypothetical Retrospection has 2 Critical Questions.

It compares actions in pairs.

If one action has greater estimation than another, it becomes the attacker.

Successors are pair-wise compared. If the attacker's successor is preferred, it attacks the defender's successor.

Utilitarianism

G(s,a) = SUM_{s'} T(s,a,s') * [J(s,a,s') + gamma*K(s')]

Absolutism

G(s,a) = OR_{s'} J(s,a,s') OR K(s').
